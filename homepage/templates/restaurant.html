At Haci Beach, we know that good food and drink are just as essential to a great vacation as stunning scenery and sunny beaches. That's why our restaurant and bar are the perfect place to refuel and recharge during your stay.

Market

For those who like to keep things simple, our little market has everything you need to stock up for a day of hiking or camping. With fresh fruits and vegetables straight from the farmers of the local village, you can be sure you're fueling your body with the best.

Restaurant

And for those who prefer a bit more variety, our restaurant offers delicious options all day long. Start your day with a typical Turkish breakfast featuring omelettes or a refreshing smoothie bowl. As you soak up the sun on the beach, enjoy a fresh sandwich or a crisp salad from our lunch menu. And when evening rolls around, indulge in the catch of the day paired perfectly with a glass of raki.

Bar

But the real star of the show? Our bar, of course. From perfectly mixed Negronis to frozen strawberry daiquiris during happy hour, our cocktails are the perfect accompaniment to a stunning sunset. And for those who need a little boost in the morning, our freshly brewed coffee is sure to do the trick.
