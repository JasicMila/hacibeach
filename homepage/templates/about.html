About: Haci Beach is not just a place, it's a vibe.

Named after the one and only Haci (/'ha:ji/) - a unique and recognizable individual - this hidden gem was discovered during one of his weekend road trips.

As a seasoned hospitality expert with years of experience running different types of restaurants and Meyhanes in the city center, Haci was yearning for a true paradise off the beaten track. And boy, did he find it! Haci Beach is now a one-of-a-kind destination that embodies his personality and passion for hospitality. So come on over and hang out at the fire pit with a glass of raki, and don't forget to have a chat with Haci. And if you want to know what he looks like, just take a glance at our logo - you'll spot him immediately!
