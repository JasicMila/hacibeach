At Haci Beach, we believe that the best way to fully enjoy the beauty of nature is to spend the night right in the heart of it.
That's why we offer a range of accommodation options that allow you to experience the pristine beauty of our location, located right on the Lycian Way.

Caravan
For those looking for a more adventurous stay, come with your own caravan. Our Caravan Park offers electricity and water connection for you. With breathtaking views of the sea and the surrounding landscape, you'll feel like a true globetrotter on the road.

Tents
And for those who prefer the pure camping experience, we offer tents that will allow you to fully immerse yourself in the beauty of our location.
With communal bathrooms and a campfire area available for your convenience, you'll have everything you need to make your stay as enjoyable as possible.

No matter which option you choose, a stay at Haci Beach promises to be an unforgettable experience. And to kick off your day of adventure, we provide all of our guests with a traditional Turkish breakfast each morning.

No matter where you're waking up you can start your day off right with a hearty and healthy breakfast at Haci Beach. So why wait? Book your stay today and start planning your dream vacation in the heart of nature.
